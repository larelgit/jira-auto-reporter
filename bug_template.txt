Title: Player falls through the bridge texture
Priority: High
Labels: collision, gameplay, regression
---
Environment: PC, Windows 11, build v0.3.42-alpha

Steps to reproduce:
1. Load the "Forest River" level.
2. Walk to the stone bridge (coordinates: X=1200, Y=340).
3. Stand on the left edge of the bridge.
4. Jump (spacebar).

Expected result:
The player lands on the bridge and stays on it.

Actual result:
The player falls through the bridge texture and falls into the water/void.
Reproduces in 8 out of 10 attempts.

Priority: High (blocks level progression).
